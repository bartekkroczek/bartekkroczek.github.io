Biography

I’m a data scientist with academic fervor. I use my technical background to solve problems in psychology and cognitive sciences. Currently, I’m mainly dealing with my doctoral dissertation on the phenomenon of behavioral oscillations.

I am also the founder of the DataGradus think tank. As part of it, I learn, research, write and talk about the scientific method and the decision-making process.

I have been a salsa instructor for several years, and in 2019 I founded a dance school in my hometown. Fell free to check us :)

I relax through sport, good cinema, meditation, books (hard sci-fi preferably), and spending time with my relatives and dogs. I have recently started learning to sing, without much success so far.

My life brings me much joy, and I wish everyone to feel the same.
